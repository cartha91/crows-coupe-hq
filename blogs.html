Jujutsu Kaisen: Season 2

Release date: July 6th, 2023

Genre: Adventure, Dark Fantasy, Supernatural


After a long and impatient wait we are finally getting what weve been waiting for. The second season of the fan favorite Jujutsu Kaisen! The second season will focus on the Shibuya Incedent with the introduction of a bunch of new faces. at the time of this article there is currently four episodes available for streaming. For those who havent started the series I highly recommend it. The series boasts a good storyline with well thought out and unique characters. There are plenty of awesome well animated fight scenes backed up with relatable and comedic drama. Jujutsu Kaisen truly offers an experience that will keep anime fans of multiple genres hooked and at the edge their seats! For those who wanna check it out Jujutsu Kaisen can be streamed on CrunchyRoll!

Rising Of The Sheild Hero

Naofumi Iwatani is one of the four legendary heroes summoned to a fantasy world to fight against the Waves of Catastrophe, unreal invasions that are full of high ranked monsters. Naofumi However, is quickly betrayed and framed by the other heros as well as the country he was summoned to protect. Quickly becoming the hated Shield Hero who is seen as the weakest and most useless of the four, With no one to trust and nowhere to run or call home, he buys a demi-human slave named Raphtalia Heals her wounds and chronic sickness and trains her to fight with him. Along the way, he meets other allies who join his cause and help him save the world from the Waves. Rising of the Shield Hero is a dark fantasy anime that explores betrayal, redemption, justice, and friendship. The anime has a great and fufilling story, characters you wont help but to love, kick ass action scenes, and beautiful animations. The anime is based on a light novel series by Aneko Yusagi and has two seasons, with a third one announced. Be Sure hop on your Filolial and head over to CrunchyRoll to Join the sheild hero and his crew!

In Another World With My Smartphone

Touya Mochizuki is a boy who is accidentally killed by God. As a way clean the slate, God allows him to be reincarnated in a fantasy world with any one wish he desires. Touya surprisingly... or maybe not so much, chooses to keep his smartphone with him, will a little questionable divine tinkering, God sets the phone to work in his new world. In his new life, Touya discovers that Magic, Monster, and Everything else you would find in something like a Final Fantasy game exist in real HD lol. Whats better than a magic world? A magic world you start off in with a ton of buffs. yea you guessed it the MC is juiced. Touya learns about the secrets and mysteries of the new world, such as the ancient civilization that left behind powerful artifacts and the threat of a dark force that seeks to destroy anything and everything. In Another World With My Smartphone is a light-hearted fantasy anime that mixes comedy, romance, adventure, and harem elements. The anime has a fun and relaxing atmosphere, with many humorous situations and references to pop culture. The anime is based on a light novel series by Patora Fuyuhara and has two seasons. If your feeling froggy hop over to CrunchyRoll to stream!
